REBOOT is a concept brand built to challenge outdated systems and celebrate personal evolution. It blends glitchwear and cyberpunk styling with bold design choices to create an identity system that feels both modern and rebellious. This passion project was entered into a design competition and includes branding, apparel mockups, and campaign materials.

Reboot is a futuristic streetwear brand that explores themes of digital rebirth, resistance to outdated systems, and the evolution of personal identity. Inspired by glitch aesthetics, minimal cyberpunk, and modern techwear, the brand combines bold typography with statement visuals and a charging-symbol motif.
The project includes a full branding suite: logo design, visual language, mockups, and an editorial-style promotional poster. Reboot was developed to be provocative, clean, and rooted in the concept of reinventing oneself in a system-driven world.
